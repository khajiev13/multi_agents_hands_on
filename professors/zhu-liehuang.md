## Basic Information
王博，男，1982年3月生，北京理工大学教授、博士生导师，国家杰出青年科学基金获得者，教育部“长江学者”特聘教授。
Wang Bo, male, born in March 1982, professor and doctoral supervisor at Beijing Institute of Technology, recipient of the National Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars, and Changjiang Scholar Distinguished Professor of the Ministry of Education.

## Research Interests
1. 新型多孔材料的设计与合成
2. 能源气体储存与分离
3. 催化与传感
4. 污染物去除
