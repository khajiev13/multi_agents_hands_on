## Basic Information
- 王业亮 (Wang Yeliang), male, born in 1979, from Shandong Province.
- Professor, doctoral supervisor.

## Research Interests
Controllable preparation, physical properties, and device applications of low-dimensional quantum materials.
1. Controllable preparation and physical property research of two-dimensional materials and their heterostructures.
2. Exploration and physical property research of novel low-dimensional quantum materials.
3. Photoelectric and spintronic devices based on low-dimensional quantum materials.

## Biography
- Joined the School of Physics, Beijing Institute of Technology in 2010.

## Education
- 2001: Graduated from the Department of Physics, Shandong University, received a bachelor's degree.
- 2006: Graduated from the Institute of Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, received a doctoral degree.

## Work Experience
- 2006-2008: Conducted postdoctoral research at Tohoku University, Japan.
- 2008-2010: Conducted postdoctoral research at the Max Planck Institute of Microstructure Physics, Germany.

## Awards
- 2013: Selected into the "New Century Excellent Talents Support Program" of the Ministry of Education.
- 2014: Received funding from the "Excellent Young Scientists Fund" of the National Natural Science Foundation of China.
- 2016: Selected as a "Chang Jiang Scholar" (Young Scholar) of the Ministry of Education.
- 2017: Selected into the National "Ten Thousand Talents Program" (Young Top-notch Talent).
- 2018: Received funding from the "National Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars" of the National Natural Science Foundation of China.
